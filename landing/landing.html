About us

Welcome to our web application Rate-My-Movie, here you can find the ability to search amongst thousands of movies. Our goal was to simplify a way of exploring and finding the best movie for you by providing ratings, reviews, basic information, genres, etc. Aside from these attributes we will be adding a weekly newsletter giving the most updated standings/statistics within the nearest 7 day period. That will include a top 10 list based on said standings and ratings, as well as a coming soon section that will advertise upcoming not yet out cinemas with the given release dates. Hope you enjoyed the page, stay tuned for a more polished interactive version coming soon!
